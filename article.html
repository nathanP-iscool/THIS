Who are we?

Teen Health, Information and Safety is an organization that targets teens mental health issues and suicide prevention. Did you know that every 1 in 2 people are impacted by suicide before they turn 25? That is absolutely devastating. As in 2020, suicide was the most common cause of death for young people aged 15-24 years. 402 young people aged 15-24 died by suicide, representing 13.0 deaths by suicide per 100,000 people. That doesn't sit right with us so we decided to make this organization to show teenagers that they belong and they're important.
